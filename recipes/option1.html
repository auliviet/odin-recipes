Camping recipes for 3 days - Option 1

Description

A selection of recipes for 3 days of camping in the woods. These recipes are designed to be simple, quick, use similar ingredients and only require limited equipment.

Breakfast

  1. Bacon & egg roll
  2. Pancakes
  3. Fruits & cereals

Lunch

  1. Vegetarian sandwich
  2. Greek salad
  3. Chicken caesar wrap

Dinner

  1. Gnocchi with pesto
  2. Haloumi burgers
  3. Fajitas with chicken

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • 8x wraps
  • 4x burger buns
  • Gnocchi

  • 1x tomato
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 1x Lebanese cucumber
  • Fruits
  • 2x red onions
  • 3x bags of leaves
  • 2x capsicum
  • 1x avocado
  • 1x lime
  • Spinach

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 eggs
  • 8 slices of bacon

  • Pesto
  • Humus
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Caesar sauce

  • Jar of roast vegetables
  • Sundried tomatoes
  • Parmesan
  • 200g feta
  • Haloumi
  • Pancake mix

Breakfasts

Breakfast 1 - Bacon & egg roll

  1. Toast the burger buns on the pan. When ready add the barbecue sauce and set aside.
  2. Cook the bacon on the pan and add to the bread.
  3. Cook the egg on the pan abd add to the bread.

Breakfast 2 - Pancakes

  1. Add water to the pancake mix following the instructions. Shake.
  2. Place the pan on the stove to warm up.
  3. When the pan is hot, start cooking the pancakes.
  4. Dice fruits to add to the pancakes.

Breakfast 3 - Fruits & cereals

  1. Dice fruits and add to the cereals.
  2. Add milk or yoghurt.
